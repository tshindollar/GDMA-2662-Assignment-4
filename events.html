Shop the Blocks is back and better than ever!

Downtown Lincoln's boutiques, book stores, art galleries, specialty shops & stores are open late and here for you. On November 17th, take your opportunity to get access to your VIP Passport to enjoy special discounts, giveaways, and more. What's different this year? Pick up your VIP Shop the Blocks bag & passport at our check in location at the Rococo Theatre (140 N 13th St.) by showing your e-ticket on your phone. The after party is back! Purchase a silver or gold tier ticket to party at the Rococo, enter to win prizes, and enjoy a cash bar. Gold tier tickets also unlock a bonus 10 entries for raffle prizes and complimentary appetizers in the gold tier lounge — order soon because these tickets are very limited!

Author Reading

  • Friday, Nov. 11th
  • 5:30 p.m. – 6:30 p.m.
  • 118 N 14th Street

Educator and Author Wendell Mcconnaha joins us at Francie & Finch Bookshop. You don't want to miss the presentation of his book The King of Lokoja: William Balfour Baikie the Forgotten Man of Africa. His book reveals the story of a largely forgotten Scottish explorer and naturalist who forged his way into West Africa and its history. This book defines the man and his accomplishments and reveals how he is so fondly remembered by the Nigerians and yet apparently so totally forgotten by the rest of the world.
