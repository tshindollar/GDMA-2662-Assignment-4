Author Reading

  • Friday, Nov. 11th

  • 5:30 p.m. - 6:30 p.m.

  • 130 S. 13th Street

Educator and Author Wendell Mcconnaha joins us at Francie & Finch Bookshop. You don't want to miss the presentation of his book The King of Lokoja: William Balfour Baikie the Forgotten Man of Africa. His book reveals the story of a largely forgotten Scottish explorer and naturalist who forged his way into West Africa and its history. This book defines the man and his accomplishments and reveals how he is so fondly remembered by the Nigerians and yet apparently so totally forgotten by the rest of the world.
